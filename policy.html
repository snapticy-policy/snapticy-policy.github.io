SnapGuide Privacy Policy

Last updated 2026-01-14
Developer Snapticy

SnapGuide (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices when using SnapGuide.

1. Information We Collect

(A) Images you provide

  • When you select or capture an image (manual/photo) to analyze, the image may be uploaded for AI processing.

(B) Device & app information

  • Basic device/app information may be collected to provide the service and improve stability (e.g., OS version, error logs).

(C) Advertising data (AdMob)

  • We use Google Mobile Ads (AdMob) to display ads (banner/interstitial).
  • AdMob may collect device identifiers and ad-related data to provide and measure ads.

2. How We Use Information

  • To analyze images and generate step-by-step guides.
  • To store guide history on your device for convenience.
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the app (stability and performance).
  • To display ads and measure ad performance.

3. Data Storage

  • Generated guide history may be stored locally on your device (e.g., AsyncStorage).
  • Uploaded images are used only for analysis purposes and are not sold.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK)
  • OpenAI API (AI analysis and generation)
  • Google Cloud Run (Backend processing)

5. Your Choices

  • You can stop using the app at any time.
  • You can remove stored history by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.

6. Children’s Privacy

SnapGuide is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
